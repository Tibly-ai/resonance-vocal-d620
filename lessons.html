Who We Teach

Every level, age nine to ninety

There's no such thing as "not a singer" here — only singers at different points on the path. Here's how we meet each one.

Level One

First-Time Singers

Never sung outside the shower? Perfect. We start with breath, ear training, and simple songs you love, building confidence before we build range. Most beginners are singing comfortably in a group setting within two months.

Level Two

Developing Singers

You sing in a choir, at church, or at open mics — and you've hit a plateau. We diagnose the technical habits holding you back and rebuild them, expanding range, stamina, and stylistic control.

Level Three

Auditioning Students

Preparing for college programs, theatre callbacks, honor choirs, or competitions. We combine technique with repertoire strategy, cut preparation, and performance psychology — with a 92% audition success rate to show for it.

Level Four

Working Professionals

Gigging vocalists, worship leaders, session singers, and touring artists. Sessions focus on vocal longevity, night-after-night consistency, and expanding your marketable range without sacrificing vocal health.
