Contact

Let's start with a conversation — and a song.

Whether you're ready to book a trial lesson, have a question about our programs, or just want to talk through whether coaching is right for you, we'd love to hear from you. We reply to every message within one business day.

Book a Trial Lesson

Your first lesson, in three easy steps

The 30-minute trial lesson is a relaxed, no-pressure introduction — $35, applied toward your first package if you continue. Available in-studio or online.

1

Send the form below

Tell us a little about your singing background and goals — even "I've never sung outside my car" counts. There are no wrong answers, and no experience is required.

2

We'll match a time

Within one business day, we'll reply with two or three trial-lesson slots that fit your schedule, along with directions to the studio or a video link if you're joining online.

3

Come sing with us

In your trial, we'll warm up together, listen to where your voice is today, and sketch a plan for where it can go. You'll leave with at least one technique you can use immediately.

Prefer to talk first? Call us at (503) 555-0187 during studio hours, Tuesday through Saturday. If we're mid-lesson, leave a message and we'll ring you back the same day.

Location & Availability

Find us in the heart of Southeast Portland

The studio sits on the second floor of the historic Harmony Lane building, a five-minute walk from the Hawthorne Bridge. Street parking is free after 6pm, and there's a paid lot directly across the street. Bus lines 14 and 15 stop within a block.

The studio is up one flight of stairs; if stairs are a barrier, let us know and we'll happily arrange your lessons online or at our partner space at ground level.
