About the Studio

Fifteen years of helping singers find their sound

Resonance Vocal Studio was founded by soprano and voice pedagogue Mara Ellison after a decade of performing and teaching across the country. What began as a handful of students in a living room has grown into a full studio serving singers from age nine to ninety.

Our approach blends classical bel canto foundations with contemporary voice science — because good singing isn't a mystery, it's a skill. Lessons are available in-studio and online, with flexible scheduling for busy students and working professionals.
